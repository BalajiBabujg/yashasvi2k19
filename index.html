ABOUT THE INSTITUTIONS

Amrita Institutions, Nagercoil campus houses Amrita College of Engineering and Technology, Amrita Polytechnic College and Sun College of Education. Amrita Institutions is managed by Mata Amritanandamayi Math, a registered charitable organization, guided by Satguru Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, a Great Spiritual Master and world-renowned humanitarian leader. She is fondly called “AMMA” by millions of Her devotees all over the world. The Math is actively involved in a wide range of humanitarian activities for the upliftment of mankind, both spiritual and material.

Mata Amritanandamayi Math has established a vast network of educational institutions across the country that offer professional education of very high quality and these institutions are ranked top in India and abroad.












ABOUT YASHASVI’19

Department of Management Studies of Amrita Institutions, Nagercoil Campus has scheduled YASHASVI’19, National Level Workshop on Digital Marketing and Events organized by on March 7-8, 2019. Launching in this year with the motive of encouraging all participants who are the pillars of the nation. The workshop and events have various management and non-management games conducted by the students. this provides them a platform to mould and express their in-built talents and knowledge.
